Hello! I'm a fifth year PhD student at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington. My research focuses on designing, developing, and evaluating novel methods and tools to help people and their health providers better understand and manage chronic health conditions. I'm advised by James Fogarty and Sean Munson. I received my undergraduate degree in computer science from Pomona College.
